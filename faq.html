Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a turf?

To book a turf, simply navigate to the booking page, select your preferred location and time slot, and follow the instructions to complete your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment gateways.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Are there any discounts available for bulk bookings?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk bookings. Please contact us directly to discuss your requirements and receive a customized quote.

How can turf owners list their venues on your platform?

Turf owners can contact our support team to discuss listing their venues. We will guide you through the registration and listing process.

What safety measures are in place at the turfs?

All our listed turfs follow strict safety and hygiene protocols to ensure a safe environment for players. This includes regular cleaning and sanitization of the facilities.

What should I bring with me when I come to play?

We recommend bringing your own sports gear, water bottle, and a towel. Some turfs may provide equipment rentals if needed.

Can I book a turf for a private event or tournament?

Yes, you can book turfs for private events or tournaments. Please contact us with your requirements, and we will help you organize your event.

How can I give feedback about a turf or my booking experience?

We value your feedback. You can leave a review on our website or contact our support team to share your experience and suggestions.

Is there a mobile app for booking turfs?

Yes, we have a mobile app available for both iOS and Android devices. Download it from the App Store or Google Play to make bookings on the go.
