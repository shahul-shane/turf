    Frequently Asked Questions

    General

    What is TurfPitch.in?

    TurfPitch.in is an online platform that allows turf owners to list their turfs, making it easier for customers to find and book them.

    Why should I add my turf to TurfPitch.in?

    Adding your turf to TurfPitch.in can increase your visibility, attract more customers, and simplify the booking and management process.

    How much does it cost to list my turf on TurfPitch.in?

    The cost structure varies; please refer to our pricing page for detailed information.

    What are the benefits of listing my turf on TurfPitch.in?

    Increased visibility, easy booking management, customer support, and promotional opportunities.

    Adding and Managing Turf

    How do I add my turf to TurfPitch.in?

    Once logged in, navigate to the dashboard and click on "Add Turf." Follow the prompts to enter the details of your turf.

    What information do I need to provide about my turf?

    Turf name, location, type of turf, size, availability, pricing, and any additional amenities or services offered.

    Can I add multiple turfs to my account?

    Yes, you can add multiple turfs under a single account. Each turf will have its own listing and management options.

    How do I upload photos of my turf?

    During the turf addition process, you will have an option to upload photos. Ensure the images are clear and showcase your turf effectively.

    Can I edit the details of my turf after listing it?

    Yes, you can edit your turf details anytime from the dashboard.

    How do I set the availability and pricing for my turf?

    In the turf details section, you can set the available time slots and corresponding prices.

    Booking and Payments

    How do customers book my turf?

    Customers can search for turfs, view your listing, and book available time slots directly through TurfPitch.in.

    How will I be notified of new bookings?

    You will receive email notifications for each new booking. You can also view bookings in your dashboard.

    How do I manage cancellations and rescheduling?

    From your dashboard, you can manage bookings, including handling cancellations and rescheduling requests.

    How and when will I receive payments?

    Payments are processed through our platform and transferred to your account according to our payment schedule. Please refer to our payment policy for more details.

    Support and Troubleshooting

    What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

    Contact our support team through the "Help" section on the website or email us at support@turfpitch.in.

    How do I contact customer support?

    You can reach our customer support via email, phone, or live chat available on the website.

    What if I forget my password?

    Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

    Miscellaneous

    Can I promote special offers or discounts?

    Yes, you can create and promote special offers or discounts through your dashboard.

    How do I get more bookings for my turf?

    Ensure your listing is complete and attractive, use high-quality images, and consider promoting your turf through our advertising options.

    What measures are in place for customer safety and satisfaction?

    We encourage turf owners to maintain high standards of cleanliness and safety. Customer reviews and ratings also help maintain quality.
