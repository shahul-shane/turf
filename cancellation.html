Cancellation Policy

At TurfPitch.in, we understand that plans can change, and we aim to provide flexibility for our customers while ensuring the smooth operation of our services. Please read the following cancellation policy carefully before making a booking.

Cancellation by Customer

Customers may cancel their booking subject to the following conditions:

  • Flexible Cancellation: For bookings canceled within 24 hours of the scheduled start time, customers will receive a full refund of the booking amount.
  • Standard Cancellation: For bookings canceled between 24 hours and 7 days before the scheduled start time, customers will receive a 50% refund of the booking amount.
  • No Refund: For bookings canceled less than 24 hours before the scheduled start time, customers will not be eligible for a refund.

Cancellation by TurfPitch.in

In rare cases where TurfPitch.in needs to cancel a booking due to unforeseen circumstances, such as maintenance or safety concerns, customers will receive a full refund of the booking amount.

Refund Process

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the booking within 7-10 business days from the cancellation date. Please note that it may take additional time for the refund to reflect in your account depending on your financial institution

Modifications

Customers may modify their booking details, such as the date and time, subject to availability and the terms outlined in this cancellation policy. Any modifications to bookings may affect the refund eligibility.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our cancellation policy, please contact us at contact@turfpitch.in. Our customer support team is here to assist you and provide further clarification if needed.
